# Blinkit Sales and Operation Analysis Project





Project Overview:


This project analyzes Blinkit operational and sales performance using Microsoft Excel and Power BI. The objective of the project is to convert raw business datasets into meaningful insights through data cleaning, structured data modeling, and interactive dashboard visualization.



Tools and Skills Used:



•  Microsoft Excel – Data Cleaning and Data Preparation
•  Power BI – Data Modeling and Dashboard Development
•  Data Analysis – Sales Analysis, Product Performance Analysis, Delivery Performance Analysis
•  Business Intelligence – KPI Monitoring and Interactive Reporting



Data Transformation Using Microsoft Excel:



•  Cleaned raw datasets by identifying missing values and correcting inconsistent data.
•  Formatted date columns and standardized column structures for proper analysis.
•  Prepared datasets such as orders, customers, products, marketing performance, delivery performance, and inventory.
•  Ensured datasets were properly structured before importing them into Power BI.



Data Modeling in Power BI:



•  Imported multiple datasets into Power BI to create a structured analytical model.
•  Created relationships between tables including Orders, Customers, Products, Order Items, Delivery Performance, Marketing Performance, and Inventory.
•  Built a Date dimension table to enable time-based analysis such as year and month filtering.
•  Configured relationship cardinality and cross-filter direction to ensure accurate data analysis.



Dashboard Development in Power BI:



•  Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize Blinkit sales and operational data.
•  Created KPI cards including Total Sales, Total Orders, Total Customers, and Total Stock Received.
•  Added slicers for Year, Month, and Customer Segment for dynamic filtering.
•  Built charts including Monthly Sales Trend, Category-wise Sales Analysis, Top 10 Products by Sales, Delivery Status Analysis, and Monthly Stock Received.



Key Insights from the Analysis:



•  Household category generated the highest sales among all product categories.
•  Top products contributed significantly to overall revenue.
•  Monthly sales trends helped identify fluctuations in business performance.
•  Delivery performance analysis revealed a high percentage of delayed deliveries.
•  Inventory analysis provided visibility into monthly stock received.



Conclusion:



This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow. Excel was used for cleaning and preparing the data, while Power BI was used for data modeling, analysis, and dashboard development. The final dashboard helps visualize business performance and supports data-driven decision making.
